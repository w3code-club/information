About Us

Welcome to W3 Code! We are a group of passionate developers, data science enthusiasts–ready to dive deep into technology and solve real-world problems through innovative projects.
We welcome creativity and innovative ideas to enhance our creative thinking skills.
Everyone is encouraged to freely discuss their ideas as we work towards the next implementation steps.
As Parami students, we value inclusivity. We respect each other's values and strive to create a community where everyone can participate and feel included.
We empower and support each other, building a non-judgmental community where everyone feels uplifted.

Activities

Overview of the activities

Activities include a kick-off meeting, Scratch programming workshop, math in programming seminar, optional simple website project, team-building activities, guest speaker sessions with professionals , a hackathon/coding competition (Scratch, Python), monthly show-and-tell, and a feedback & reflection session.

Our Mission

At W3 Code,Our mission is to empower Parami’s students who have an interest in programming and boost their skills by fostering a collaborative community that promotes the students’ continuous learning, and problem-solving. :
